About the game

Arachnid Descent is a 1–3 player cooperative survival escape game where you must work together, find all the scattered pieces while avoiding the threat that is constantly lurking around every corner, and earn your way out. The daunting, carefully crafted environment adds to the intense and immersive experience. Players can take on the challenge alone or team up with friends — either way, the quality of the experience remains the same. Collectible coins can also be found throughout the map and used to purchase stylish, rare skins right here on our website. We hope you have a great time with the demo — good luck, and we welcome any constructive feedback!
